Easy Healthy Tacos: Try this recipe for your next meal!

Recipe of the Week, Dec 6, 2020

Ingredients: 1lb Ground Turkey, Onions, Protein Tortillas, Spices: Cumin, Paprika, Oregano, and Salt Toppings: Low-Fat Cheese, Avocado, Tomato, Salsa, Cilantro, and Green Onion

1. Heat up pan and saute onions.
2. Add meat and spices and cook until brown.
3. Place cooked meat inside tortillas and top with your favorite toppings.

Leg Day: 3 sets // 12 reps

This weeks workout, Dec 6, 2020

This workout can be done with or without dumbbells. It is recommended for beginners to start without weights, gradually working your way up in dumbbell weights.

1. Dumbbell Sumo Squats + Sumo Dead Lift
2. Dumbbell Split Squat, each leg
3. B-Stance Dead Lifts, each leg
4. Side Glute Bridges, each side + Frog Pumps

It's healthy to sleep: why sleeping is so important for your wellness

Article of the Week, Dec 6, 2020

From the article linked below, you can read why it is so important for you to get a good night sleep. We took the top 5 reasons and posted them below.

1. Poor sleep is linked to higher body weight
2. Good sleepers tend to eat fewer calories
3. Good sleep can imporve concentration and productivity
4. Good sleep can maximize athletic performance
5. A poor sleep schedule can lead to depression
